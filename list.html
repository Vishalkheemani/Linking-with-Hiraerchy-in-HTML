List

  1. ol = Ordered List:

    Ordered lists are lists where each item in the list is numbered.

    Example:
    1. Vishal Kheemani
    2. Manu Yadav
    3. Bhalji Bheel
  2. ul = Unordered List:

    Unordered lists are lists that begin with a bullet point.

    Example:
    • Adnan
    • Ahmad
    • Amjad
  3. dl = Definition List:

    Definition lists are made up of a set of terms along with the definitions for each of those terms.

    Example:
    Science:
    Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation, experimentation, and analysis.
    Math:
    Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns, and the relationships between them.
